Grungle is a quirky card game based on the rock, paper, scissors format.
- A grid is generated from a deck of 21 cards.
- The goal of the player is to accumulate points by selecting cards to battle.
- The outcome of each card battle is resolved through some simple rules.
- The 21 card deck is made up of 3 suits. The Dragon, The Kraken and the Unicorn.
- Dragon beats Kraken. Kraken beats Unicorn and Unicorn beats Dragon.
- Each card from each suit has a value ranging from 1 to 7.
- If the winning cards value is higher than the losing card, the difference in value is added as points to the player.
- If the winning cards value is lower than the losing card, the difference is deducted in points from the player.
- If the cards are the same suit. Then no points are won or deducted.
- After each battle, the cards are removed from the board.
